Tea

Description.

Tea is a widely consumed food across Nigeria. It is made from tea leaves. Sugar, honey, milk or chocolate may be added for enhanced and alternative taste.

Ingredients

  • Tea leaves or Tea bags
  • Boiling Water
  • Optional; Sugar, Milk, CChocolate, Cream

Steps

  1. Bring one cup of water to boil.
  2. Turn the boiling water in a cup
  3. Steep the tea bags/tea leaves in the water and stir gently with a spoon for a minute.
  4. cover the cup for 5 to 10 minutes.
  5. Remove the tea bag ( or filter the tea leaves out)
  6. Optional: Add Sugar, Milk, Chocolate to taste
