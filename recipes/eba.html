Eba

Description.

Eba is a widely consumed food across Nigeria. It is made from garri which is prepared from cassava. It is easy to prepare and is very filling.

Ingredients

  • Garri
  • Boiling Water

Steps

  1. Bring two cups of water to boil.
  2. Turn three quarters of the water in a bowl.
  3. Add the garri little by liitle making sure to distribute it evenly in the water
  4. Do this till the garri completely covers the garri. Add what is left of the boiling water.
  5. Turn the garri with a ladle till the consistency is even. Add water or garri till the desired texture is reached.
